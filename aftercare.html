Aftercare

Brows

  • FIRST DAY : No makeup or skin care on your eyebrows for 7 days
  • Do not work out in the first 48h
  • Do not get them wet at the first day.
  • Do not get sun exposure
  • Blot with a tissue until the lymph liquid stop coming out from the eyebrows.
  • SECOND THROUGH THE SEVENTH DAY :
  • AFTER CARE CREAM will be provide.
  • WASH your eyebrows and hands with neutral/ antibacterial soap
  • DRY your eyebrows with a paper tissue
  • APPLY a thin layer of the after-care cream to the eyebrows 2 times a day. (antibiotic cream or ointment)
  • Use sterile bandage(s) or other sterile dressing(s) when necessary.

Lips

  • No makeup or skin care on your lips for 7 days
  • Do not work out in the first 48h
  • Do not get sun exposure
  • You may ice your lips as needed to avoid sweeling
  • Take Arnica in tablets 3 days before and 7 days after procedure (3 times a day)
  • WASH your mouth with mouth washer couple times a day for 5 days.
  • AFTER CARE CREAM will be provide.
  • WASH your lips and hands with neutral/ antibacterial soap
  • DRY your lips with a paper tissue
  • APPLY a thin layer of the after-care cream to the lips couple times a day for 5 days.
  • Use sterile bandage(s) or other sterile dressing(s) when necessary.
Eyeliner
  • No makeup or skin care on your eyes for 7 days
  • Do not work out in the first 48h
  • Do not get them wet
  • Do not get sun exposure
  • You may ice your eyes as needed to avoid swelling
  • APPLY Tears Moisture Eye Drops.
